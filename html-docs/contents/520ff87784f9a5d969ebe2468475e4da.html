TradeAddress

UMLClass
TradeAddress

Description

The location at which a particular trade related organization or person may be found or reached.

Generalized Elements

  • Address

Attributes

Visibility Name Type Description
public CareOf Text

The name, expressed as text, of a person or organization at this trade address to whom incoming mail is marked with words such as 'care of' or 'C/O'.
public Identification Identifier

A unique identifier for this trade address.
public AttentionOf Text

The name, expressed as text, of a person or department in the organization to whom incoming mail is marked with words such as 'for the attention of' or 'FAO' or 'ATTN' for this trade address.
public BuildingName Text

The name, expressed as text, of a building, a house or other structure on a street at this trade address.
public AdditionalStreetName Text

The additional name of a street, expressed as text, for this trade address.
public CitySub-DivisionName Text

A name, expressed as text, of a sub-division of a city for this trade address, for example a district or borough.
